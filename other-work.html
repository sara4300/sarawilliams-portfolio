Who's Sara?

I grew up as a student athlete. Lacrosse was my world for most of my life, and competing at a high level shaped the way I work more than any class ever did. I learned to thrive under pressure, to trust the people around me, and to put in the unglamorous preparation that makes the visible work look easy.

Outside of design, I'm drawn to anything that involves making something: photography, video, travel. I find a lot of creative energy in noticing the world around me and capturing it. Kaia, my dog, is my favorite subject.

I care about work that matters. Healthcare, government systems, the tools that real people rely on when the stakes are high. That's where I want to be, designing things that are worth getting right.
